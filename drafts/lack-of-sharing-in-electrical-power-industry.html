Lack of Sharing in Electrical Power Industry

Permalink: 2015-09-03 00:00:00-05:00 by Keith Gray in work tags: work open-source

I am currently at the Pacworld Americas conference in Raleight, NC. I have really enjoyed the conference, attended some really interesting presentations, and been engaged by the discussions.

I am sure everyone who is attending is seeing different threads in the industry. It has been really interesting to me to see all of the discussion of IEC 61850 and the interoperability it allows. Some hardware vendors have fully embraced it and some are still fighting it. Either way, it is clear that the industry wants all of the equipment working together.

Everyone seems to agree that IEC 61850 contains really great features and makes it possible to implement schemes that were not possible without it. However, it also introduces new problems. A couple of presentations spoke specifically about the Engineering process used by IEC 61850.
